Product Stacks

CitruxPay offers 3 product stacks

  • Payments
  • Banking
  • UPI

Payments stack

A single API to initiate fund transfers via IMPS, NEFT, RTGS and UPI to any bank account number or UPI ID and check the status of the fund transfers

Banking stack

Create unique virtual accounts for your customers and instantly receive transaction alerts when funds are added to the virtual accounts through UPI, NEFT, IMPS, RTGS.

UPI stack

Issue UPI IDs and QR codes to any merchant for accepting payments through UPI and receive instant transaction callbacks
